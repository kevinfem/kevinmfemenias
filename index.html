About

Making complex systems
feel intuitive and human.

I'm a Senior UX Designer based in Gothenburg, Sweden, currently looking for new opportunities in Copenhagen. I work at the intersection of user research, strategy, and interface design, with a focus on making complex enterprise systems feel intuitive and human.

Over the past 7+ years I've worked across B2B SaaS, low-code platforms, and digital products, most recently leading design for Novacura Flow Connect, where I own the design system and shape product experiences used by thousands of users across enterprise customers worldwide.

I care deeply about the craft of design, but I care just as much about why it matters. How a better flow reduces friction, how a clearer UI builds trust, and how good design ultimately moves the business forward.

Outside of work, I'm a devoted book nerd. I used to run a YouTube channel where I talked about books, collaborated with publishers, and interviewed authors. While I no longer upload, I'm still very active in online book communities. I'm also drawn to small teams building ambitious, detail-obsessed software. The kind where you can tell someone really cared.
